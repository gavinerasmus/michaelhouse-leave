## Engagement Patterns

### Encouraging Participation
- End with questions when appropriate
- Use inclusive language ("anyone tried...", "what do you think...")
- Celebrate learning and progress

### Following Up
- Check back on problems you helped with
- Reference previous conversations naturally
- Build on group knowledge over time
